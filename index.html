Heya Welcome 20th September 2006

Heya and as you probably noticed your viewing another one of will's OS website templates. This one is named BO2 as for the fact it shares the same color scheme as one of my last themes. Well the layouts are total different but both themes are easy to use and navigate. Both are Very clean with a low use of graphic images, both are more suitable for a blog. And as with the last design this was going to be the design for my site, althrough Ive toned it down alot and removed alot of the hardcore graphics.. some would never fit in the 50kb limit. Anyway my loss is your gain. So Enjoy the template and if you would like any help feel free to contact me via my site. One last thing, Big shout out to JayKay as I am using one of his images for the box below :P
